Frequently Asked Questions

Is FileFlow Pro really free?

Yes! All basic file conversions are completely free. We support ourselves through ads and optional donations.

Are my files safe?

Absolutely. Your files are processed in your browser and never stored on our servers. We take privacy seriously.

What file formats do you support?

We support 30+ formats including images (JPG, PNG, WebP), documents (PDF, TXT), and more. See our home page for the complete list.

How long does conversion take?

Most conversions happen in seconds. Larger files might take a bit longer, but we're optimized for speed.
